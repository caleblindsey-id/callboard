Key Design Decisions

  • Period toggle — switches all sections between weekly and monthly views
  • KPI cards — team-wide aggregates with period-over-period delta
  • Leaderboard — sortable by different metrics (revenue, tickets, profit, efficiency). Red highlight row = below target threshold
  • Trend chart — monthly bar chart with actual vs projected. Toggles between revenue, tickets, and profit margin
  • Click-through — any tech row links to /analytics/[id] for the deep-dive
