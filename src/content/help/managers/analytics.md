The **Analytics** section shows how the team and each technician are performing, and lets you set targets to measure against.

## Team view

Open **Analytics**. Toggle **Weekly** or **Monthly**, and read the KPI cards:

- **Tickets Completed**
- **Total Revenue**
- **Gross Profit**
- **Avg Hours/Ticket** (lower is better)
- **Avg Completion** days (lower is better)

Below the cards, the **leaderboard** ranks technicians — sort by revenue, tickets, profit, or efficiency — and a **trend chart** shows the team over time.

## Technician deep-dive

Click a technician to open their profile: a scorecard, a trend chart with their target line overlaid, a revenue breakdown (PM vs. service), and their recent tickets.

## Set targets

Tap **Team Targets** (team view) or **Set Targets** (a tech's profile) to set goals for tickets completed, revenue, revenue per hour, and average completion days. Actuals show red / yellow / green against the target.

## Gotcha

**Gross Profit only appears once a technician's hourly cost is set** (in the Users list — see [Provision a user](/help/admin/provision-a-user)). Without it, profit shows blank because there's no cost to subtract.
