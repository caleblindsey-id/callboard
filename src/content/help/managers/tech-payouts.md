Once leads have earned and ACE labor is approved, the **Tech Payouts** page is where you total it up for a pay period and mark it paid.

## Pull the payout report

1. Open **Tech Payouts** and go to the **Payout Report** tab.
2. Set the date range with **From (earned date)** and **To**. It defaults to the **prior calendar month**.
3. Tick **Include already-paid** if you want to see what's already been paid; leave it off to see only what's owed.

The summary shows the totals in range and the payout period derived from the **To** date (year-month).

## Export and mark paid

- **Export CSV** downloads everything in range — lead bonuses and ACE labor — for payroll.
- To mark paid: tick the **earned** rows, tap **Mark paid (n)**, and confirm the period. The rows flip to paid and drop off.
- **ACE labor** has its own section and its own **Mark ACE paid (n)** button — paid separately from lead bonuses.

## Gotchas

- **The default range is last month, not this month** — adjust the dates if you're paying a different period.
- Only **earned** (unpaid) rows can be selected to mark paid; already-paid rows are read-only.
