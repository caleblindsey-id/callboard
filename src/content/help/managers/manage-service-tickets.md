A service ticket can be left unassigned when it's created, and the technician can be changed at any time afterward. Both the single-ticket control and the bulk board action are available to managers and office staff (coordinators); technicians can't reassign work.

## Reassign a single ticket

On the service ticket's detail page, the **Assignment** card sits near the top:

1. Pick a technician under **Assigned Technician** (or choose **Unassigned** to clear it).
2. Tap **Save**.

The "Assigned to …" line updates immediately. **Save** stays disabled until you actually change the selection.

## Assign in bulk from the board

From the **service board**, you can assign several tickets to one technician at once:

1. Tick the checkbox on one or more tickets (use the header checkbox to select all in the current view).
2. In the action bar that appears, choose a technician under **Assign to…** and tap **Assign**.

The selection clears and the board refreshes with the new assignee. Bulk assign isn't available in the **Deleted** view.

## Note

Only active technicians appear in the assignment lists. Assignment changes the technician only — it doesn't move the ticket's workflow status (open, estimated, approved, and so on stay as they are).
