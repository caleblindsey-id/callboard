If a requested part is no longer needed — warranty covered direct by the vendor, wrong part, customer withdrew — cancel it from the **Parts Queue**.

## How to cancel

1. In the [Parts Queue](/help/office/order-parts), find the part's row.
2. Tap the red **✕** on that row.
3. In the **Cancel part request** dialog, enter a **Reason** — this is **visible on the ticket**, so keep it factual (e.g., "Warranty covered direct by vendor").
4. Tap **Cancel request**.

A reason is required — the button stays disabled until you enter one.

## What happens to a cancelled part

- It **drops off** the To Order / Ordered / Received tabs and no longer counts as a part the ticket is waiting on.
- It **stays on the source ticket**, shown struck-through with your reason, so there's a record of why it was dropped.

## Note

You can't cancel a part that's already been **received** — by then it's in hand and tied to the work.
