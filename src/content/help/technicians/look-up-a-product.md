Need a part number or a price while you're with a customer? The **Products** section is a searchable, read-only copy of the catalog, synced nightly from Synergy.

## How to look something up

1. Tap **Products** in the menu.
2. In the search box, type a **product number** or part of a **description** (for example, "seal kit" or "12345").
3. Results appear as you type. Each shows the item number, description, and unit price.

That's it — the catalog is read-only here, so there's nothing to save or change.

## Good to know

- Search matches either the item number **or** words in the description, so you don't need the exact number.
- Pricing and items come from Synergy. If something's wrong or missing, it gets corrected in Synergy and updates here on the next nightly sync.
- The list shows when each item was **last synced**, so you can tell how current it is.
