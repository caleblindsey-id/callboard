The **Equipment** section shows the machines you've been assigned to through PMs and service tickets, sorted so the most urgent work floats to the top.

## Finding a machine

1. Tap **Equipment** in the menu.
2. Search by **customer name** or **serial number** to filter the list.

Each machine shows the customer, make/model, serial, location, last service, and **next service** — color-coded so you can prioritize:

- **Red "Overdue"** — the PM is past due.
- **Amber** — due this month.
- **Gray** — scheduled for the future.

Tap any machine to open its detail page.

## What you can do on the detail page

The equipment details, PM schedule, and service history are **read-only** for you — but two things you *can* do:

- **Update the on-site contact** (name, email, phone) and tap **Save Contact** when it changes.
- **Add a note** — common part numbers, quirks, access instructions, gate codes. Notes are timestamped and visible to everyone who works the machine.

## Tips

- Work the red (overdue) machines first — that's how the list is sorted by default.
- If you can't find a machine, it's probably not assigned to you yet. It'll appear here once you're on a PM or service ticket for it.
