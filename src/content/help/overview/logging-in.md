CallBoard lives at **callboard.services**. Open that address in your phone or computer's web browser. On a phone, add it to your home screen so it opens like an app.

## Signing in

1. Go to **callboard.services**.
2. Enter your work email and password.
3. Tap **Sign in**.

## Your first login

When your account is created, an admin gives you a temporary password. The first time you sign in, CallBoard will require you to set your own password before you can do anything else. Choose something only you know, then continue — you'll land on your dashboard.

## Quick PIN (faster sign-in)

After you sign in with your email and password, CallBoard offers to set a **quick PIN** for that device. Once it's set, you only enter a 4 to 6 digit PIN to sign in on that device instead of your full email and password.

A few things to know:

- Your PIN works **only on the device where you set it**. It never replaces your password, and your password always still works.
- On a **shared shop device**, several people can each set their own PIN. The sign-in screen shows a "Who's this?" list so you tap your name, then enter your PIN.
- Manage your PIN any time from **Account Security** at the bottom of the navigation menu: set it, change it, or remove it from the current device.
- **Lost or replaced your phone?** Ask a manager to reset your PIN access. That clears your PIN from every device, and you sign in with your password again (then set a fresh PIN).

If you enter the wrong PIN too many times, PIN sign-in locks for a while. You can always tap **Use password instead** to get in.

## Forgot your password

1. On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password**.
2. Enter your work email.
3. Check your email for a reset link and follow it to set a new password.

If the reset email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder, then ask an admin to confirm your email address is correct in CallBoard.

## Changing your password later

You can change your password any time from the **Change Password** link at the bottom of the navigation menu.

## Staying signed in

CallBoard keeps you signed in on a device, so you normally won't have to log in every day. If you use a shared computer, use **Log Out** at the bottom of the menu when you're done.
