CallBoard is the system we use to run service operations end to end — scheduling and completing preventive maintenance (PMs), handling service tickets and customer estimates, ordering parts, tracking sales leads, billing completed work, and reporting on technician performance. It replaces the old EasyBee software.

CallBoard runs in your web browser at **callboard.services**. There is nothing to install. It works on a phone, tablet, or computer — technicians do most of their work from a phone in the field.

## What CallBoard does

- **Preventive Maintenance (PMs)** — generates the month's recurring PM tickets, routes them to technicians, and captures the completed work order with photos and a customer signature.
- **Service tickets** — reactive repair work, from the first call through the customer estimate, approval, completion, and billing.
- **Parts ordering** — a shared queue where the office orders parts for any open ticket and tracks them from requested to received.
- **Tech leads & payouts** — technicians submit new-PM and equipment-sale leads; approved leads earn bonuses that roll into a monthly payout.
- **Billing** — turns completed PMs and service tickets into itemized PDFs for invoicing in Synergy.
- **Analytics** — technician and team KPIs: tickets, revenue, profit, and efficiency.

## How CallBoard relates to Synergy

Synergy is still the system of record for customers, products, and invoicing. Every night CallBoard pulls the latest customers, contacts, products, and ship-to locations from Synergy. That means customer and product information in CallBoard is **read-only** — if something looks wrong, it gets fixed in Synergy and flows over on the next sync.

Billing works the other way around: you complete work in CallBoard, then enter the resulting invoice in Synergy. CallBoard records the **Synergy order number** so the two systems stay tied together.
