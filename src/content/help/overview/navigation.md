CallBoard is organized into sections you reach from the **navigation menu**.

- On a **computer**, the menu is always visible down the left side.
- On a **phone**, tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner to slide it open, then tap a section.

What you see in the menu depends on your role — technicians see a shorter, field-focused list. See [Roles and permissions](/help/overview/roles-and-permissions) for who sees what.

## The sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your home screen — today's work and what needs attention. See [The dashboard](/help/overview/the-dashboard). |
| **Preventive Maintenance** / **My PMs** | The PM ticket board — recurring maintenance work. |
| **Service Tickets** | Reactive repair work — estimates, approvals, and completion. |
| **Credit Review** | Customers on credit hold awaiting a manager decision before billing. |
| **Parts Queue** | The shared list of parts to order, ordered, and received across all tickets. |
| **Tech Payouts** | Approved lead bonuses and ACE labor, exported for payroll. |
| **Billing** | Completed work ready to be invoiced in Synergy. |
| **Equipment** / **My Equipment** | Machines under PM, their details, default parts, and service history. |
| **My Leads** | Technician-submitted leads for new PMs and equipment sales. |
| **Prospects** | Inactive equipment flagged as a sales opportunity. |
| **Customers** | Customer records, contacts, and ship-to locations (synced from Synergy). |
| **Products** | The product catalog with pricing (synced from Synergy). |
| **Analytics** | Technician and team performance KPIs. |
| **Settings** | App configuration — admins only. |
| **Audit Log** | A record of who changed what — admins only. |

## At the bottom of the menu

- **Help & Guides** — this manual.
- **Change Password** — update your own password.
- **Log Out** — sign out (use this on shared devices).

## The feedback button

A **Send Feedback** button floats in the bottom-right corner on every screen. Use it any time something is confusing, broken, or missing — it goes straight to the team.
