The dashboard is the first screen you see after signing in. It's your starting point each day — a summary of what's open and what needs attention. Tap **Dashboard** in the menu to return to it any time.

What the dashboard shows depends on your role.

## If you're a technician

Your dashboard is focused on your own work in the field:

- **Your assigned tickets** — the PMs and service tickets currently on your plate.
- **Parts on order** — parts that have been ordered for your tickets but haven't arrived yet.
- **Parts ready for pickup** — parts that have arrived, so you know what to grab before heading out.

You only ever see your own tickets and parts — not other technicians' work.

## If you're office staff or a manager

Your dashboard gives a wider operational view across the branch — open and overdue work, items waiting on a decision, and totals that help you see where the day stands. From here you drill into any section to act.

## Tips

- **Overdue PMs** are highlighted in red. Clear these first.
- Anything waiting on **you** specifically (an approval, a credit decision, a skip request) is what to act on before it becomes a bottleneck for someone else.
- The dashboard reflects live data — pull it up again after you finish a batch of work to see what's left.
